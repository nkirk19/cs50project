About this Project

Welcome to our final project for CS50: Introduction to Computer Science at Harvard College. In this program, we attempt to use the skills and concepts we learned throughout the fall 2019 semester to collect, graph, and analyze data. All of us enrolled as first-years in CS50 with little to no prior coding experience, thus this semester has been both challenging and exciting. We all completed the web track.

In our project, Trends in Voting: 2016 Presidential Election Edition, we were interested in looking at the significance of factors such as poverty and education on voting trends. Through choropleth maps and correlation graphs, we sought to understand whether there was a correlation between data related to demographics and party. As you navigate the website, keep in mind trends you found surprising in the data. See if some of your thoughts are noted in our observations and conclusions!
